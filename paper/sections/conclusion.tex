\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

This paper presented \textbf{AgriSense}, a comprehensive AI-powered agricultural intelligence platform that synergistically combines ensemble machine learning models with generative AI to deliver a holistic farming decision support system. The platform's Random Forest Classifier achieves 99.32\% accuracy on crop recommendation across 22 crop classes, while the Random Forest Regressor attains an $R^2$ score of 0.9641 for yield prediction. The integration of Google Gemini 2.5 Flash for generating contextual, natural-language insights represents a novel contribution that enhances the interpretability and practical utility of ML predictions for non-technical end-users. Complementary modules for profit analysis, heuristic-based risk assessment, and comparative crop profitability evaluation further enrich the decision-making toolkit.

Built on a modern, scalable architecture using FastAPI and React/Vite, AgriSense demonstrates the feasibility and value of integrating classical ML, generative AI, and contemporary web technologies to address real-world agricultural challenges. The platform serves as a foundational blueprint for next-generation precision agriculture systems that are not only accurate but also accessible, interpretable, and actionable. Future work will focus on incorporating deep learning, IoT integration, multilingual support, and advanced risk modeling to further enhance the platform's capabilities and reach.
